# Changelog

## 0.1.2-beta (House Ads Migration & Dynamic Feed/Article Promotion Engine)

- **Complete AdMob Removal & Self-Hosted Ad Migration**: Stripped all Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads (`play-services-ads`) dependencies, tracking SDKs, and third-party ad configurations. Transitioned 100% of promotional delivery to self-hosted, privacy-friendly **House Ads** served from `https://house.monkey-network.xyz/api/public/ads`.
- **Asynchronous House Ad Repository (`HouseAdRepository`)**: Created a dedicated, thread-safe repository (`Dispatchers.IO`) to fetch and parse JSON ad payloads into rich `HouseAd` domain models. Includes package name validation against `BuildConfig.APPLICATION_ID` (`com.monkeybytes.budgienews`) and robust fallback handling when offline or unreachable.
- **Dynamic Interspersed Feed Placement (`NewsList`)**: Upgraded campaign loading from a single static top-of-feed banner (`.firstOrNull()`) to comprehensive multi-campaign state management (`houseAds: List<HouseAd>`). Banners now automatically intersperse into the news stream after every 4th story (`itemCount % 4 == 0`). When multiple active campaigns exist, the feed dynamically cycles through them (`activeAds[adIndex % activeAds.size]`), giving balanced impressions to every promotion during scrolling.
- **Article Summary Page Integration (`StoryDetail`)**: Embedded `HouseAdBanner` directly onto the individual story summary view beneath the `QuickRead` card. Utilizes deterministic campaign hashing (`(item.id.hashCode() and 0x7FFFFFFF) % activeAds.size`) to ensure each article summary displays a stable, consistent promotion without layout jitter or recomposition flicker.
- **Native UI/UX & Badge Polish**: Replaced third-party commercial `"Sponsored"` / `"Tap to open sponsored link"` phrasing with clean, native `"Promo"` and `"Tap to learn more"` badges (`AccentSoft` styling), harmonizing house campaigns seamlessly with the app's modern news design language.
